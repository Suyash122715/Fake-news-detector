🧠 How It Works

What is Fake News?

Fake news refers to misinformation or hoaxes spread via traditional news media or online social platforms. It can be created to mislead people, push political agendas, or attract clicks for profit.

Why Detect Fake News?

  • Prevents spread of misinformation
  • Protects public opinion and democracy
  • Promotes media literacy and truth

How Does Our Detector Work?

Our Fake News Detector uses machine learning to analyze news content and determine its authenticity based on various linguistic, statistical, and contextual features.

The process involves:

  • Data Collection: Trained on real and fake news datasets
  • Text Preprocessing: Cleaning and converting news into analyzable format
  • Model Prediction: A classifier (like Logistic Regression, SVM, or Deep Learning model) decides if news is real or fake

This tool is designed to assist users in making informed decisions, but it is not a substitute for fact-checking from trusted sources.
